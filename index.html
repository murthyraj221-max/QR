Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FlexQR

How do I create a WiFi QR code?

▼
Use the WiFi format: WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;;

Can I use a custom logo?

▼
Yes! Upload PNG or JPG logos. The logo will be automatically placed in the center of your QR code. Adjust the size using the slider.

Is my data secure?

▼
Absolutely! All QR code generation happens locally in your browser. We don't store, track, or upload any data to servers.

What formats can I export?

▼
You can download QR codes as high-quality PNG images. Vector export is also available through the copy function.
