# AdSense launch checklist for FlexQR

1. Use a custom domain instead of a free subdomain.
2. Replace the support email with a real domain email.
3. Replace the placeholder `ads.txt` file with the official AdSense line after approval.
4. Keep the privacy policy and terms links visible in the footer.
5. Make sure all pages load cleanly on mobile and desktop.
6. Verify the site in Google Search Console and submit `sitemap.xml`.
7. Add original content pages over time so the site does not feel thin.
